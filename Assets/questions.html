Name the six subjects every Hogwarts student is required to study during their first two years of school. Godric’s Hollow. 8. In Book 7, what three Ministry of Magic employees do Ron, Harry, and Hermione impersonate? Mafalda Hopkirk, Reginald Cattermole, and Albert Runcorn. 9. What spell does Harry discover written in the Half-Blood Prince’s potions textbook in Book 6, and who invented it? Sectumsempra, Severus Snape. 10. What do the abbreviations for the two main wizarding exams, O.W.L.s and N.E.W.T.s, stand for? Ordinary Wizarding Level and Nastily Exhausting Wizarding Test.
